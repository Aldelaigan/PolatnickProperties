Polatnitck Properties Clients

A FULL SERVICE FIRM

Polatnick Properties is a specialty commercial real estate company that has been serving the Bay Area since 1989. We specialize in the disposition, acquisition, redevelopment and repositioning and management of office, specialty use complexes, industrial and retail properties throughout California, with a focus on the San Francisco’s Bay Area

Why us

Our diverse clientele includes property owners, high net worth individuals, REITs, national investment firms, corporations and large public institutions. The defining common factor between them all, and the reason we are proud to have them as partners and clients, is that they are seeking innovative, comprehensive and strategic solutions to their real estate needs. From uncovering valuable opportunities, through negotiating due diligence and navigating the permit process, to the realization of each client’s vision; our team acts strategically with creativity, efficiency and diligence.

How we work

We begin by developing a complete analysis of each client’s short and long-term financial goals to create an individualized marketing and development strategy. Our extensive, in-depth knowledge of competitive leasing and sales product and market activity guarantees informed decision making and no missed opportunities. Our long-standing relationships with local experts such as Land Use Attorneys, Architects, Engineers, Contractors, Expeditors, and Political Consultants allow us to compose a team of highly qualified professionals for each project. All of these elements ensure the best possible positioning in alignment with the clients’ objectives, thereby maximizing return on investment and asset value.

Our team has unparalleled experience in San Francisco’s commercial marketplace. Through many years in the business, we have built an extensive database of contacts, including national and international investors, developers and property owners, as well as access to the entire brokerage community.

What makes us special

Our work has included the redevelopment and repositioning of several notable city properties, as well as other long-term, multi-faceted projects that included the acquisition and development of property for high-profile Bay Area Institutions. We are best known for our ability to buy, reposition and sell “gems in the rough”; identifying each property’s highest and best use, and creating a strategic plan to reposition it as such. We believe in creating, rather than simply presenting opportunities for our client. We skillfully maneuver through and welcome the challenges that are part of any redevelopment opportunity.

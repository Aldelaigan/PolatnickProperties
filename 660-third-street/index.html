660 Third Street

660 Third Street, San Francisco, CA 94107

IN THE HEART OF SOMA

660 Third Street is in the heart of the SOMA District which has become the “In Spot” for fine dining, entertainment and major retailers. The location offers excellent transportation options; one block to CalTrain, Muni Light Rail/bus lines and easy access to Hwys 101, 80 and 280. It is also within walking distance of the Embarcadero Waterfront and a stone’s throw away from AT&T Ballpark, which provides entertainment events throughout the year.

Classic Brick and Timber, newly renovated 11,864 SF space in SOMA includes 6 private offices, 5 conference rooms and meeting rooms, as well as operable windows with daylight streaming in from three sides with views of downtown. The building is secure with a 24/7 card access system, security cameras and a front door intercom system. Call for pricing. Lease is direct with the landlord. On-site parking is available.

Terms are negotiable. Landlord is looking for a 5 to 10 year lease.
